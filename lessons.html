Claireville Ranch is located close to the city. We offer Western riding lessons. Whether you are new to riding or you would like to brush up on your old skills, we have programs available to suit a variety of ages and skill levels.

Safe Rider Program

Available: Saturday & Sunday Mornings

Designed specifically for the beginner or novice rider. Students will learn how to safely groom, tack, handle and ride horses. Once graduated from the program, students may enter our lesson program. Minimum age for the program is 10 years old, and all riders must also have a 27inch inseam.

  • Price: 400$ per person for the full month program
  • Length: Four 2 hour lessons, one each Saturday or Sunday (your chosen day for the month)
  • Terrain: Outdoor arena, and on our trails
  • Pacing: Lessons progress from groundwork and groom & tack, to riding in the arena through the month.
  • For: Begginners and novices - riders must be above 10, have at least a 27" inseam, and be under 230 pounds

Lesson Program

Available: Saturday & Sunday (call or email to check for available classes)

Group, semi-private or private lessons. OE Rider certificates can be earned. You must complete the safe rider program, or pass a riding exam if you have prior experience, before being enrolled into lessons.

  • Price: $70 per person (group) or $75 per person (semi-private), purchased four at a time
  • Length: Approximately 1 hour
  • Terrain: Outdoor arena, grass arena & trails if arena is unstuitable due to mud
  • Pacing: Based on individual riders, walk/trot/lope
  • For: All skill levels - riders must be above 10, have at least a 27" inseam, and be under 230 pounds
  • Due to a lack of indoor facilties, lessons do not run during the winter
