Pony Ranch Experience

Available: Weekends and Holidays, 8:45am - 9:15am & 9:15am - 9:45am To Book:  Call (905-794-0700) or email Clairevilleranch@gmail.com Location: 3805 Queen St E, Brampton, ON ‚ù¶ L6T 0B3

(Pony Ranch Blurb)

Details

  • Price: 45$ per pony slot, with a maximum of two children per pony
  • Length: 30 minutes
  • Terrain: Enclosed pony paddock; with grass, tree, and small hill
  • Activites: Paper worksheets, grooming, and riding (walk only) - as your child wishes
  • For: Children under 10 - must be old enough to hold themselves up in the saddle. We recommend no younger than 2-3
  • Pony rides must be booked & paid in advance

Things To Know

  • There are two ponies in the pen at a time, and a total of four slots per weekend day
  • Closed toed shoes (running shoes, boots, etc) are required, and we reccommend long pants for your child's comfort
  • Helmets will be provided - if you bring your own, it must be an equestrian helmet (no bike/ski/hockey helmets) within its best before date
  • If you wish to be around the pony yourself, you will also require close toed shoes
  • If you're bringing multiple children, please ensure they stay safely out of the way if not with a staff member!
